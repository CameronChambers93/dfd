About Us

A Michigan company built on one simple belief: you deserve a job well done.

Our Story

For 43 years, our owner worked in water damage mitigation — helping homeowners recover after the worst days of their lives. That career built a foundation of one non-negotiable principle:

"You Deserve A Job Well Done."

The pivot to dumpster rental came from a single eye-opening incident. While on a job in Bloomfield Hills, a traditional metal-wheeled roll-off dumpster was placed in a customer's driveway. What it left behind wasn't just debris removed — it was a deeply gouged, cracked driveway that cost the homeowner thousands to repair.

That moment sparked a question: why does getting rid of junk have to damage your property?

The answer was rubber-tire dumpsters — a newer technology that distributes weight evenly, levels on slopes, and fits into spaces roll-offs simply can't. In 2015, Driveway Friendly Dumpster was born.

The business validated itself quickly. One of the first customers pointed to a 10-foot gouge on his cement driveway left by a roll-off competitor, then looked at our equipment and said, "You're going to be busy."

He was right. Today we serve 40+ communities across Michigan's tri-county area — and not one of our customers has ever had to file a driveway repair claim.
